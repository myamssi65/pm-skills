# Resource Bottleneck Audit




Identify and unblock team-level resource constraints before they impact delivery timelines.




## Overview




In complex execution environments, delays are rarely caused by a single task but rather by "resource bottlenecks" - specific roles, tools, or individuals that are over-leveraged across multiple workstreams. This skill provides a structured method for PMs to audit resource allocation, identify hot spots, and re-balance workloads to maintain velocity.




## Execution Steps




1. **Map Cross-Team Dependencies**: Identify all workstreams currently active and the resources (people, infrastructure, budget) they share.
2. **Quantify Load vs. Capacity**: For critical resources, calculate the percentage of time allocated to high-priority tasks versus "maintenance" or "support" tasks.
3. **Identify Single Points of Failure**: Highlight any resource that, if unavailable for 48 hours, would stop more than one major workstream.
4. **Draft a Re-balancing Plan**: Propose shifts in allocation, such as delaying low-priority tasks or temporarily embedding specialists in the bottlenecked area.




## Key Deliverables




- **Resource Heatmap**: A visual representation of over-utilized vs. under-utilized resources.
- **Bottleneck Impact Analysis**: A brief report on how current constraints affect end-of-quarter goals.
- **Intervention Roadmap**: Actionable steps for leadership to resolve the bottleneck (e.g., hiring, tooling, or scope reduction).
